Help Center

RTA VTC - Royal Thai Army Video Teleconference

How to Join a Meeting

1 Open RTA VTC app
2 Enter the room name in the text field
3 Tap "Join Meeting" button
4 Configure your microphone, camera, and audio-only settings in the pre-join dialog
5 Tap "Join" to enter the conference

Settings

Tap the settings icon (top right) to:

  • Display Name - Set your name shown to other participants
  • Default Audio - Set microphone on/off by default
  • Default Video - Set camera on/off by default
  • Clear History - Remove all meeting history

Meeting History

Recent meetings are shown on the main screen. Tap any room name to quickly rejoin that meeting.

During a Meeting

  • Mute/Unmute - Tap the microphone icon
  • Camera On/Off - Tap the camera icon
  • Screen Share - Available from the overflow menu
  • Chat - Tap the chat icon to send messages
  • Leave - Tap the red phone icon to leave the meeting

Troubleshooting

  • Cannot connect - Check your internet connection and try again
  • No audio/video - Make sure you granted camera and microphone permissions in your device settings
  • Poor quality - Try switching to audio-only mode for better stability
