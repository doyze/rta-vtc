Privacy Policy - RTA VTC

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Introduction

RTA VTC ("the App") is a video teleconference application developed by the Royal Thai Army for official use by military personnel. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Camera and Microphone

The App requires access to your device's camera and microphone to enable video and audio communication during conference sessions. This data is streamed in real-time and is not recorded or stored by the App.

Display Name

The App allows you to set a display name for identification during meetings. This is stored locally on your device only.

Conference Room Name

The App collects the conference room name you enter to connect you to the appropriate meeting session. Recent meeting history is stored locally on your device for convenience.

Network Information

The App uses your internet connection to facilitate video conferencing. No network traffic data is stored or analyzed beyond what is necessary for the conference session.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide real-time video and audio conferencing services
  • To connect you to the correct conference room
  • To maintain the quality and stability of the conference session
  • To display your name to other meeting participants

Data Storage and Security

  • The App does not store personal data on external servers beyond the duration of the conference session.
  • All conference communications are transmitted through Royal Thai Army servers (telemeet.rta.mi.th).
  • User preferences and meeting history are stored locally on your device only.
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect data during transmission.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the Jitsi Meet SDK, an open-source video conferencing platform. Please refer to the Jitsi privacy policy for additional details regarding the underlying conferencing technology.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Conference data is only accessible to participants within the same conference room.

Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by Royal Thai Army personnel and is not directed at children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated revision date.
